Executive Summary: Token Lebnani

The Operational Blueprint & Regulated Architecture

Token Lebnani functions as a regulated infrastructure bridge, engineered to facilitate secure cross-border investment into Lebanese real estate. By utilizing the Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM) as a legal wrapper, we convert physical property rights into liquid, digital securities protected by English Common Law.
View Master Architecture Tree

1. The "Regulated Bridge" Architecture

Our model relies on a sophisticated dual-jurisdiction structure designed to isolate risk and ensure enforceability:

  • Layer 1 (The Platform) Token Lebnani Ltd Operates as the regulated interface, applying for an FSRA Category 4 (Arranging) license to structure the deals.
  • Layer 2 (The Issuer) ADGM-domiciled Holding Company Issues the digital tokens. This "Bankruptcy Remote" vehicle owns the Lebanese subsidiary, keeping assets separate from platform risk.
  • Layer 3 (The Asset) Lebanese Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) Holds the physical title deed. This ring-fences the asset, ensuring legal issues in one property cannot affect others.

The Legal Link: A "Look-Through" legal structure, architected by YazConsult, ensures that token holders possess enforceable rights to the underlying assets. This is reinforced by Binding Arbitration clauses under the New York Convention, ensuring ADGM judgments are enforceable in Lebanon.

2. The 3-Step Execution Process

We transform brick-and-mortar assets into digital securities through a streamlined lifecycle:

I. Acquisition The Lebanese SPV acquires a Grade-A asset. The SPV owns the property before any tokens are sold to investors, ensuring Zero Counterparty Risk (Asset-First Model).
II. Tokenization The ADGM Holding Company issues 100% of the tokens to the Asset Owner initially. These are programmable securities on the blockchain.
III. Distribution Investors purchase tokens via a secure dashboard. Net Operating Income (rent) is calculated by the FMC and distributed via Direct Bank Transfer (USD) to minimize regulatory friction.

3. Key Operational Advantages

Secondary Market Access: Unlike traditional private equity, we provide a pathway to liquidity from Day 1 by listing tokens on partner ADGM-licensed Multilateral Trading Facilities (MTFs).

Non-Custodial Architecture: We operate a non-custodial model. Investors hold their own private keys (e.g., MetaMask, Ledger). Token Lebnani never holds client funds or assets, significantly reducing regulatory capital requirements and increasing trust.

High-Touch Concierge: We bridge the technical gap for HNWIs with a "White-Glove" service that provides Guided Wallet Setup and personal onboarding, removing the "tech barrier" for traditional investors.

4. Institutional Governance

To ensure bank-grade compliance and operational speed, we leverage best-in-class partnerships:

  • Compliance: Waystone acts as the outsourced Money Laundering Reporting Officer (MLRO), ensuring 100% AML/CFT compliance.
  • Technology: Digishares provides the ISO 27001-compliant, institutional-grade tokenization infrastructure.
  • Legal: YazConsult manages the cross-border structuring and enforceability opinions.

Financial Model: The company operates on a "Recoverable Onboarding Expense" model, where setup costs are pre-financed or fully recoverable from initial transaction fees, imposing no long-term debt on the platform.

5. Go-To-Market Strategy: The High-Ticket Concierge Model

To navigate the ADGM Sandbox constraint of a 200-investor cap, Token Lebnani eschews the high-volume retail model in favor of a "Digital Private Bank" approach. We treat every investor slot as premium real estate.

Ticket Size & Revenue

$65,000+ (Strict Qualification: All investors must verify Net Assets > $500,000 to qualify as Professional Clients). Target: Maximizing revenue per slot to achieve our $10M target within Sandbox limits.

The Execution Engine

A B2B2C "Force Multiplier." We scale without a massive salesforce by leveraging trusted intermediaries (Real Estate Agencies in Lebanon, Wealth Advisors in GCC).

Operational Backbone: Zero-Friction Onboarding

To address the "Legacy Tech Barrier" of our non-technical demographic:

  • The Concierge Role: A dedicated Client Services Coordinator handles 100% of the "tech friction" (KYC, Guided Wallet Setup, TPA Signing) via personal Zoom/WhatsApp support.
  • The "Shadow" Close: Our Head of Capital Partnerships ("The Hunter") shadows the client relationship from the first handshake to the final wire transfer.
  • Farming Strategy: Post-acquisition, we focus on maximizing "Share of Wallet" by offering existing investors first refusal on new assets.
