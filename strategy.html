Step 1: Asset Sourcing & Contract Execution

WHAT
Token Lebnani identifies, evaluates, and signs an exclusive agreement with a property owner to tokenize their asset.
WHY
Token Lebnani operates as a facilitator, not as an asset owner. This exclusive contract is the trigger that initiates the entire tokenization process.
HOW
The Head of Asset Origination negotiates terms including the percentage of ownership to be fractionalized, valuation methodology, and fees. Only after this binding agreement is signed does the structuring process begin.

Step 2: Your Investment Gets a UAE Legal Shield

WHAT
We create a UAE-based Holding Company (in Abu Dhabi Global Market) that will own the property.
WHY
This establishes the legal foundation that will ultimately hold the asset under English Common Law protection.
HOW
Token Lebnani incorporates the Holding Company under ADGM regulations, establishing the regulatory framework that governs investor rights and asset protection.

Step 3: The Asset Is Secured

WHAT
The Lebanese property is legally transferred to a dedicated Lebanese Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV - a Lebanese registered company).
WHY
This "Asset-First" model ensures the SPV owns the physical title deed before any tokens are sold, eliminating counterparty risk.
HOW
The property deed is transferred through a Lebanese notary into the SPV's name, with the ADGM Holding Company registered as the sole owner. Legal validation ensures compliance with Lebanese foreign ownership rules.

Step 4: Professional Managers Handle Everything

WHAT
The ADGM Holding Company appoints a professional Facility Management Company (FMC) to manage the property.
WHY
Investors are passive financial beneficiaries—they should not manage tenants, repairs, or taxes. The FMC ensures professional operations and reliable income collection.
HOW
The FMC signs a legally binding Facilities Management Agreement with the Lebanese SPV. This contract mandates rent collection, property maintenance, tax payments, and monthly reporting to the Holding Company.

Step 5: Token Lebnani Cannot Access Your Tokens—Ever

WHAT
100% of the ownership tokens are initially issued to the property owner, then the pre-agreed percentage becomes available for sale on the Token Lebnani platform.
WHY
Token Lebnani operates as a regulated facilitator—never taking ownership of the asset or tokens. The platform simply enables the property owner to fractionally sell their equity through a secure, automated process.
HOW
Token Lebnani creates digital securities using institutional-grade technology. These tokens are special-purpose digital certificates sent directly to the owner's personal digital wallet. Token Lebnani has no ability to freeze, transfer, or control your assets.

Step 6: Tokens Are Offered to Qualified Investors

WHAT
Token Lebnani lists the tokens on its regulated platform for sale to Professional Clients.
WHY
This opens access to diaspora capital while complying with regulatory requirements—Token Lebnani never acts as a fund or exchange.
HOW
The property owner authorizes Token Lebnani to arrange the sale. Investors view the offering via a secure dashboard, complete identity verification, and are whitelisted in the system. Funds settle via a regulated Escrow Agent.

Step 7: You Acquire Direct Economic Rights

WHAT
You purchase tokens that represent legal equity in the ADGM Holding Company—and thus pro-rata ownership of the Lebanese SPV and physical asset.
WHY
You are not buying a promise or a derivative—you are buying enforceable property rights.
HOW
You sign two binding instruments: 1) A Token Purchase Agreement (TPA) governed by ADGM law. 2) A Smart Contract that records your rights. Funds are wired in USD; tokens arrive in your wallet.

Step 8: If a dispute arises, international law protects you

WHAT
Your ownership is protected by Binding Arbitration under the New York Convention.
WHY
If a dispute arises, an ADGM judgment can be enforced directly against the Lebanese property—bypassing slow local courts entirely.
HOW
The Lebanese SPV's Articles of Association include Share Pledges and arbitration clauses. ADGM courts recognize your token ledger as the definitive shareholder register.

Step 9: Earn, Trade, & Access

WHAT
You receive quarterly income distributions, can trade tokens on regulated trading venues, and transfer ownership instantly upon death.
WHY
This solves the core Lebanese problems: illiquidity, probate delays, and opaque cash flows.
HOW
Earn: Rental income is collected by FMC.
Trade: Tokens compatible with regulated venues.
Inherit: Simple recovery process for heirs.
